DETAILS OF THE TRAJECTORY DATA:
===============================

This folder contains trajectories whose radial coordinate (r) is within 50-100km during the interval 0-10ms.

Following are the column numbers and data-kind in a trajectory file.


col.| qty.
==========

1.  Time(ms)
2.  r(km)
3.  theta(deg)
4.  x(km)
5.  z(km)
6.  rho(g/cm^3)
7.  P(dyne/cm^2)
8.  Ye
9.  velocity of the SPH particle(km/s).
10. T(MeV)
11. electron chemical potential(including electron rest mass).
12. proton chemical potential(without rest mass).
13. neutron chemical potential(without rest mass).
14. bin-enu density(Electron neutrino density estimated using bin-data)(cm^{-3}).
15. bin-enu mean energy(MeV).
16. bin-enubar density(cm^{-3}).
17. bin-enubar mean energy(MeV).
18. ray-enu density(enu density computed using ray tracing)(cm^{-3}).
19. ray-enu mean energy(MeV).
20. ray-enubar density(cm^{-3}).
21. ray-enubar mean energy(MeV).
22. ray-xnu density(cm^{-3}).
23. ray-xnu mean energy(MeV).
